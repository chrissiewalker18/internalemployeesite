Welcome to The Amazing Race: One Advisory Board Edition!

Across H1, individuals in Delivery and Consulting & Management departments will race around the globe by way of eight epic prize locations. Players gain distance by passing OAB leads, scheduling OAB visits, and securing OAB units.

Here's How You Play:
  • Throughout H1, you will be competing to earn race points to advance to various locations on the Amazing Race map called race legs.
  • OAB metrics occurring between 1/1/16 and 6/30/16 are eligible for race points.
    OAB leads = 1 race point, OAB visits = 3 race points, OAB units = 5 race points.
  • Race points will advance you to the next race leg, and a new prize location will be revealed.
  • On EOB June 30th, the contestant in race leg eight with the highest number of race points will win a five day trip to any of the eight prize locations they traveled through during the Amazing Race!
