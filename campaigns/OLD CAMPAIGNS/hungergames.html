The Office Hunger Games

"The rules of the Office Hunger Games are simple...

the Advisory Board has selected several offices to participate as tributes. These six offices will be placed in a competitive arena of lead passing. Over a period of five weeks, the tributes must pass leads to stay in the games. The last office standing wins."
How Will The Games Work?
  • Each office's performance will be tracked by average leads per passer for that week.
  • The lowest performing office will be eliminated.
  • Surviving offices will continue, but metrics and rankings will reset for the new week.
  • One office will be left standing as the games' victor!
Surviving the Arena
  • Each office will have a mentor to help by providing updates and resources to the office during the games.
  • Offices can also receive a "Parachute" from an executive sponsor allowing them to earn extra leads in a short period of time.
  • The only way to guarantee survival is to pass leads!
Win a Customied Victor's Village for Your Office!

OAB will reward the winning office with a Victor's Village for the entire office to enjoy! We've made a list of 10 awesome items. The winning office will have a budget of $1,000 to spend on any of these prizes:

Foosball Table, Wii U and Games, Espresso Machine, Popcorn Maker, Stocked Wine Fridge, Soft Serve Ice Cream Machine, Sports Package for Office TV, Massage Chair, 2-in-1 Air Hockey and Billiards Table, Indoor Golft Putting Set
