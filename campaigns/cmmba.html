SO YOU WANT TO GET YOUR MBA?

Presented by One Advisory Board, this MBA program is safely nestled between Harvard and Yale on the Forbes list of top places to get your MBA (just look closely). The program is designed to help you, the student, navigate the big world of consulting here at the Advisory Board. We know it can be intimidating, so we’ve enlisted a top notch group of faculty and TAs to aid you in your pursuit of knowledge. The best part? Once certified, you are eligible for .75% of the new business resulting from the OAB leads you pass to the Consulting & Management team, in addition to regular OAB incentives.

Professors include: John Johnson, Laurie Sprung, Dennis Weaver, Anthony D’Eredita, Trenor Williams, Eric Passon, Jim Bonnette, Meredith Damore, Karen O’Hern, and more.

TAs Include: Rachel Krasnow, Sarah Walsh, Rachel Lord, Jane Roberts, and more.


CLASS SCHEDULE

Because the world of Consulting & Management is so vast, we’ve broken up the classes into seven possible areas of concentration: Hospital Performance Management, Value Based Care, Patient Access, Financial and Operations, Practice Management, Strategy, and Clinovations. Hungry for more? Find all the meaty resources on Box.

The MBA program will now feature a two-part series: the first being an overview of one of the seven areas of focus, and the second being a roundtable discussion of relevant member problems with one of the MBA professors. We will host MBA sessions for individual teams during In-Days or regular team meetings.


FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What if I want to get MBA certified but my team doesn’t have an upcoming session?
Start the process by filling out the “Request a MBA Session” button in the upper right hand corner of this page or reach out to Jane Roberts.

What if I want to set up a MBA session for my team, but I’m not sure which area of C&M is most relevant to us?
Click the button above that says “Request a MBA Session”, and we will be in touch to help you figure out what will best resonate with the member or prospect you interact with regularly.

What if I was qualified earlier in 2015 for MBA incentives by passing the quiz?
You’re set through the end of the year! In 2016, you will need to get your MBA recertified (and you have to get recertified every calendar year).

How long does my MBA incentive eligibility last?
You are eligible for the calendar year in which you gain your eligibility, with the exception of Q4 2015 in which case you will be eligible for the remainder of 2015 and all of 2016.

When does my incentive eligibility kick in?
As soon as you complete both sessions and complete the homework, you are immediately eligible to receive incentives. Use the OAB lead tracker to make sure you submitted a lead and that it’s been tracked.

Where can I find additional C&M resources?
Need something specific? Reach out to The OAB Team. If you just want to explore, find resources on Box, or here on the One Advisory Board Website.
