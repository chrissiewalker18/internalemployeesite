The 2016 OAB Marketer Ascent

Across Q2 2016, marketers are racing upwards by passing top tier leads. How high will your vertical climb?

Game Rules:
  • From April 1 – June 30th, all Marketers will compete as members of their “MEC Team” – a Marketing Lead and all of his or her Marketers compete as one team.
  • Each qualified* lead passed by a Marketer is worth 1 point to his or her team.
  • Each qualified lead for a product inside their vertical count for 2 points. For teams that don’t have such opportunities, we will pair with one other similar team for product cross-sell (i.e. EAB RI groups).
  • At the end of the campaign, the team with the highest average point total per marketer will win the 1st place prize.

Prizes:

1st Place, highest point average:
Team Dinner at a top DC Restaurant during in-days in July

2nd Place, highest lead average:
Choice of catered team breakfast or team happy hour during July in-days
*If the team with the highest point average is also the team with the highest lead average, the 2nd place prize will go to the team with the second highest lead average.
