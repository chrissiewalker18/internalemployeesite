Sean Sullinger
Associate Director

Sean started life at the Advisory Board in January 2011 as a Marketing Associate, and joined the Strategic Marketing team in February of 2012. Until April of 2015, he worked on the product marketing team, first as a product marketing manager for various PT and Consulting products, and then leading the product marketing team for the Margin vertical. Now he leads ABC’s One Advisory Board efforts. Sean hails from the great and beautiful state of California where he grew up learning how to generally be awesome. He then moved to DC for college (Hoya Saxa!!) and has been here ever since. Sean will happily join you any time for a refreshing run around DC, kayaking adventure on the Potomac, or pint of a tasty India Pale Ale (or whatever you are pouring).

Reach out to Sean for:

  • General questions about One Advisory Board
  • Questions about lead crediting or incentives
  • To talk tactics around generating leads
  • If you want OAB to present at a team meeting or in-day
