SO YOU WANT TO GET YOUR MBA?

Presented by One Advisory Board, the MBA program is safely nestled between Harvard and Yale on the Forbes list of top places to get your MBA (just look closely). The program is designed to help you, the student, navigate the big world of consulting here at the Advisory Board. We know it can be intimidating, so we’ve enlisted a top notch group of faculty and TAs to aid you in your pursuit of knowledge. The best part? Once certified, you are eligible for .75% of the new business resulting from the OAB leads you pass to the Consulting team (up to $2,500).

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What if I want to get MBA certified but my team doesn’t have an upcoming session?
Start the process by filling out the “Request a MBA Session” button in the upper right hand corner of this page or reach out to Jane Roberts.

What if I want to set up a MBA session for my team, but I’m not sure which area of Consulting is most relevant to us?
Click the button above that says “Request a MBA Session”, and we will be in touch to help you figure out what will best resonate with the member or prospect you interact with regularly.

What if I was qualified earlier in 2015 for MBA incentives by passing the quiz?
Your qualification ended on 12/31/2016! In 2016 and beyond, you will need to get your MBA recertified (and you have to get recertified every calendar year).

How long does my MBA incentive eligibility last?
You are eligible for the calendar year in which you gain your eligibility.

When does my incentive eligibility kick in?
As soon as you complete both sessions and complete the homework, you are immediately eligible to receive incentives. Use the OAB lead tracker to make sure you submitted a lead and that it’s been tracked.

Where can I find additional Consulting resources?
Need something specific? Reach out to The OAB Team. If you just want to explore, find resources on Box, or here on the One Advisory Board Website.
